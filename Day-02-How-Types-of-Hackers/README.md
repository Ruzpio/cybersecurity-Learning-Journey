## Day 2 — Types of Hackers

**Topic:** Hacker Classifications  
**Date:** 17/3/2026

### What I Learned
- **White Hat** – Ethical hackers. Operate legally with permission.
- **Black Hat** – Malicious hackers. Operate illegally for personal gain.
- **Grey Hat** – No explicit permission, but no malicious intent.

### Lab
- Microsoft paid ONE person $200,000 for a single bug report in 2024. Google paid out $12 million total.
- These are White Hat hackers — and that's the career I'm building toward

### Key Takeaway
White Hat hacking is a legitimate, well-paying career. 
Bug bounty hunters earn real money finding real vulnerabilities — legally.

Ethical Hacker / Penetration Tester
