## Day 1 — The CIA Triad

**Topic:** Foundations of Cybersecurity
**Date:** 16/3/2026

### What I Learned
The CIA Triad is the foundational model for information security:
- **Confidentiality** – Only authorized users access data
- **Integrity** – Data remains accurate and unaltered
- **Availability** – Systems are accessible when needed

### Lab
- I researched the 2021 Facebook breach. 533 million records exposed. 
- Identified CIA violation in the breach: **Pure Confidentiality violation**

### Key Takeaway
Every cyberattack breaks at least one pillar of the CIA Triad.
